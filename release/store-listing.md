## 🇬🇧 English (en-US)

### Short description (≤ 80 chars)
```
Read, listen to recitation, bookmark verses, track khatam — no ads.
```

### Full description (≤ 4000 chars)
```
Surau is a calm, fast, ad-free Qur'an app — built to keep you focused on the reading.

READ ALL 30 JUZ
• Crisp Arabic text in the Uthmanic Hafs (KFGQPC) font.
• Optional Indonesian translation and Latin transliteration.
• Adjustable font size, light/dark theme, and reading layout.

LISTEN TO RECITATION
• Play verse-by-verse or a full surah.
• Choose your preferred reciter.
• The current verse is highlighted and the screen follows along.
• Control from the notification and lock screen — keeps playing in the background.

BOOKMARK & NOTE
• Mark important verses and add your own notes and tags.
• Manage every bookmark from one screen.

TRACK YOUR PROGRESS
• A 30-juz khatam tracker with history.
• Daily streaks and a reading-activity map to stay consistent.

OFFLINE
• Download the entire Qur'an to read without a connection.
• Verse search keeps working offline.

PRIVACY FIRST
• Usable as a guest, no account required.
• No ads, no trackers, no third-party analytics.
• Create an account only if you want to sync progress across devices. Delete your account any time from inside the app.

Available in Indonesian and English. Free.
```
